Flame Trees Limited

Privacy statement

We collect your personal information in order to conduct research and consulting activities that we have been contracted to carry out by third parties.

If you access any of our services (e.g., as a client engaging us to carry out research and consulting services, as a participant in research surveys / interviews / focus groups), we will collect some or all of the following personal information:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Your address or region
  • Your correspondence with Flame Trees Limited staff
  • Demographic information such as date of birth, age, gender, and ethnicity
  • Survey results and responses, when you participate in any of our surveys
  • Interview and focus group recordings and transcripts, when you participate in any of our interviews and / or focus groups
  • Any other information you choose to provide us.

We may also collect personal information from third parties where it is necessary for us to carry out the research and consulting services we have been contracted to complete.

We keep your information safe by storing it in encrypted files on cloud servers and only allowing certain staff to access it. We keep your information for 6 years after completing the work for which the information was collected, at which point we securely destroy it by securely erasing all digital copies.

You have the right to ask for a copy of any personal information we hold about you, and to ask for it to be corrected if you think it is wrong. If you’d like to ask for a copy of your information, or to have it corrected, please contact us at info@flametrees.co.nz.
