Competition prizes

There will be one (1) winner who will receive one (1) x NZD$50 Prezzy® card.

Competition period: The competition runs from 9:00 AM 23 August 2021 until 9:00 AM 6 September 2021.

How to enter

To enter the competition, entrants must complete the “Learning from ex-participants of Auckland club rugby” Survey 2021 (distributed by Flame Trees Limited) and supply their contact details (via the survey) during the competition period. One entry per individual.

Who can enter the competition

Entry is open to all invited survey respondents who are ex-participants in Auckland club rugby (where “Auckland club rugby” refers to the competitions and clubs that form the Auckland Rugby Union). Employees and contractors of the Auckland Rugby Union are not eligible to enter the competition.

Notification of winners

The prize winner will be notified via phone or email by 30 September 2021 (the Notification).

Once notified, the winner must accept the prize within one (1) week.

The winner will be selected by a random draw from all entrants who are eligible.

A prize will be forfeited and Flame Trees Limited reserves the right to offer the prize to the next eligible runner-up winner if:

  • If a winner does not accept the prize within one (1) week of being notified by Flame Trees Limited.
  • Flame Trees Limited is unable to contact a winner within 5 working days of being notified.
  • A winner is not eligible to apply or breaches these terms and conditions.
  • A winner has already been drawn as a winner.

The prize will be issued via courier to the winner to their selected postal address as confirmed by them when notified as a winner.

No financial or other consideration will be awarded to any forfeiter.

No exchanges / cash alternatives

The prize must be taken as stated and cannot be exchanged or altered in any way. There will be no cash alternatives. The Prezzy® card is non-transferable and must not be resold.

Personal information submitted by all entrants (including name, email address and postal address) will be collected and used for the purposes of conducting this competition, in accordance with our Privacy Policy accessible here.

You may contact Flame Trees Limited by phone or email to request access to, or correction of, any of your collected personal information that is held by Flame Trees Limited.
